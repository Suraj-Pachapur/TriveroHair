# WHO WE ARE

TRIVERO exists to help people understand their hair before they attempt to treat it.

We believe that healthy hair begins with understanding the body—not chasing products.

We are building a scientific assessment platform that transforms confusion into clarity.

---

# MISSION

Most people treat hair loss.

We help them understand it first.

---

# TAGLINE

Know your problem from its roots.

---

# PURPOSE

To make scientific hair assessment accessible, understandable and actionable for everyone.

---

# VISION

To become the world's most trusted platform for understanding hair health through science, not assumptions.

---

# BRAND PROMISE

Every assessment should leave the user knowing more about themselves than before they started.

We do not promise miracles.

We promise understanding.

# WHAT WE BELIEVE

Hair is an outcome.

The body is the source.

Every person's hair tells a story.

Understanding should always come before treatment.

Good science is calm.

Truth builds trust.

Trust builds long-term relationships.

Education creates better decisions.

Small improvements consistently applied create meaningful change.

We are not an oil company.

We are not a cosmetic brand.

We are not a miracle solution.

We are not an AI company.

We are not a replacement for dermatologists.
