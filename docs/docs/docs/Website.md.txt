Hero Illustration Specification

The Hero illustration is a core brand asset and must immediately communicate scientific credibility, precision, and premium quality.

Requirements

• The hair follicle illustration must be anatomically accurate and scientifically realistic.

• The illustration should be rendered in a premium 3D editorial style, combining scientific visualization with modern product-design aesthetics.

• The artwork should resemble a high-end medical visualization rather than a textbook diagram.

• Hair shafts should appear healthy, naturally textured, glossy, and realistic with subtle variations in thickness and curvature. Avoid perfectly straight or artificial-looking strands.

• Skin layers, follicles, sebaceous glands, and blood vessels should be clearly recognizable while remaining visually clean and elegant.

• The illustration should emphasize depth, realistic lighting, soft shadows, and physically accurate materials.

• Use soft ambient lighting with warm ivory reflections and subtle gold highlights that align with the TRIVERO brand palette.

• Avoid cartoon illustrations, flat vectors, low-poly models, or generic stock medical graphics.

• The illustration should feel comparable to Apple's product renders combined with Nature journal scientific visualizations.

• The illustration should occupy approximately 55–60% of the Hero section on desktop while maintaining generous whitespace.

Animation Requirements

The Hero illustration should move naturally rather than appearing static.

Use subtle premium animations only.

• Slow floating motion (±6px vertical movement)

• Gentle breathing scale (1 → 1.02 → 1)

• Soft mouse-responsive parallax

• Subtle lighting shimmer across the hair shaft to simulate healthy reflective cuticles

• Hair strands should exhibit extremely subtle natural sway, as if responding to a gentle airflow (avoid exaggerated movement)

• Animations must run continuously without distracting the user.

Performance Requirements

• Use hardware-accelerated transforms.

• Maintain 60 FPS on modern devices.

• Optimize the illustration for Retina and high-resolution displays.

• Use optimized image formats (WebP or AVIF) or a high-quality 3D render when appropriate.

The Hero illustration should become a recognizable visual identity of TRIVERO and should never appear generic or stock.
